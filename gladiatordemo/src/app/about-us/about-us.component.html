About Larsen and Toubro Infotech Limited

Larsen and Toubro General Insurance Co. Limited (formerly known as Larsen and Toubro Infotech Limited), is a global IT solutions and services company based in Mumbai, India. In 2017, NASSCOM ranked LTI as the sixth-largest Indian IT services company in terms of export revenues.

Larsen and Toubro Infotech Limited, It was among the top 15 IT service providers globally in 2017, according to the Everest Group's PEAK Matrix for IT service providers.It employs standards of the Software Engineering Institute's (SEI) Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) and is a Maturity Level 5 assessed organization. Founded as L&T Information Technology Ltd in December 1996, LTI is a wholly owned subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Ltd (L&T).
